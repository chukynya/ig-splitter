Tutorial: How To Split

  1. Upload a PNG, JPG, or WEBP image.
  2. The image appears immediately in Live Preview below the form.
  3. Change split mode, rows, and columns to see real-time preview updates.
  4. Click Split Image when the preview looks correct.
  5. Download all tiles in one ZIP, or select specific tiles and download selected ZIP.

Downloads follow your browser settings. Most browsers save into the Downloads folder by default.
